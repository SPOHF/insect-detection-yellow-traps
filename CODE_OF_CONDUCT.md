# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give constructive, specific feedback.
- Focus on technical issues, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or exclusionary language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive or hostile conduct

## Scope

This applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, code review, and direct project communications.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to project maintainers. Include:
- what happened
- where/when it happened
- involved accounts
- supporting evidence (links/screenshots)

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled confidentially where possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or block participation for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.
